Contrast

All Recipes

www.allrecipes.com/

Drawing in the eye is the main purpose of using contrast. There are main ways of applying the the principle of contrast. For instance, the contrast could be between a small font size and a large font size. Here the designer utilized the principle of contrast by using large font size(title of the recipe) and a small font size (description of the recipe).

Proximity

Los Angeles Times

https://www.latimes.com/

The website utilized the principle of proximity by grouping related items together - the news title and the items that are related to it.

Alignment

Abbotsford Convent Foundation

https://abbotsfordconvent.com.au/visit/

The website utilized the principle of alignment by using left alignment. This way, you can see the invisible line that connects all the items on the page into one cohesive unit.
