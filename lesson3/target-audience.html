Target Audience

  • Businesses and organizations based in Cape Town who want to advertise and promote their products.

Personas

  • A startup company has developed a mobile application and they want to host an event to promote the app.
  • An organization has branched off within Cape Town and they want to notify their customers through email.

Scenarios

  • A customer wants see a list of all the educational organizations in Cape Town.
  • A customer wants to know more about a certain business.
  • A customer want to see people's testimonies about a certain organization.
  • Someone wants to be a member of the Chamber.
  • A customer want to see all the locations of a certain organization.
